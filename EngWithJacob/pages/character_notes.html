Character Database

King Edric
Ruler of Silver Kingdom

The grieving king. He lost his wife, Queen Elara, years ago. He is strict, traditional, and values clarity. He holds the kingdom together but is haunted by the past.

Queen Marwen
The New Queen / Antagonist

Mysterious and manipulative. She uses the ancient Mirror to spy and control. She seeks to consolidate power and eliminate threats to her rule.

Lyria
The Protagonist

A young woman with a mysterious past and dormant magical powers. She is awakening to her destiny and is hunted by the Queen's forces.

Eva
Healer & Guide

A wise woman living in the forest. She hides Lyria and helps her understand her powers. She is a connection to the old ways.

Grenn
Bandit Leader

A dangerous bandit blocking the road. He is well-equipped with black leather armor, a longsword, and a silver dagger.
